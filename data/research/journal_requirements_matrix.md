# Journal Requirements Matrix

## Research Date: 2025-01-18

This document contains submission requirements for target journals that publish systematic reviews in health informatics and digital health domains.

---

## Category A: Health Informatics/Digital Health Journals

### JMIR (Journal of Medical Internet Research)

**Submission Format:**
- File formats: DOC, DOCX, RTF, or PDF
- LaTeX: Accepted as PDF only
- No strict page limits initially (formatting required after acceptance)

**Abstract:**
- Structured abstract required
- Sections: Background, Objective, Methods, Results, Conclusions
- Word limit: ~250 words

**Citations & References:**
- In-text citations: Square brackets, numerical order [1], [1,3,18]
- Consecutive citations: Hyphen [2-13]
- Reference style: AMA Style preferred (but not strictly required at submission)
- References automatically processed by RefCheck system
- PMIDs recommended for PubMed articles

**Figures:**
- Upload separately from text
- Formats: TIFF, EPS, PNG, JPEG
- Resolution: Minimum 300 DPI
- Color: Accepted
- Captions: Required, detailed

**Tables:**
- Editable format (Word table tool, Excel)
- Embedded in main document
- Must be cited in text
- Legends required

**Required Sections:**
- Abstract (structured)
- Keywords (5-10)
- Introduction
- Methods
- Results
- Discussion
- Acknowledgments (recommended)
- Funding (required)
- Conflicts of Interest (required)
- Data Availability (required)
- Authors' Contributions (recommended)
- Abbreviations (required)

**PRISMA Requirements:**
- PRISMA 2020 compliance expected for systematic reviews
- PRISMA checklist may be required as supplementary material

**Special Requirements:**
- ORCID IDs required for all authors
- Author information entered separately in submission system (not in manuscript)
- Multimedia appendices supported
- Preprints accepted (must cite if published)

**Open Access:**
- Hybrid journal
- Article processing charge: ~$2,000-3,000 USD

## Category B: IEEE Journals

### IEEE Systems Journal

**Submission Format:**
- File formats: PDF (initial submission)
- LaTeX: Preferred format (IEEEtran.cls template)
- Final submission: LaTeX or Word source files required
- Two-column format, font 10 Times Roman

**Abstract:**
- Unstructured abstract
- Word limit: ~150-250 words
- Must be self-contained
- No abbreviations, footnotes, or references

**Citations & References:**
- IEEE style (numbered, square brackets)
- In-text: [1], [1]-[3]
- References: Author, "Title," Journal, vol., no., pp., year.
- Format: Last, First Initial. "Title," Journal, vol. X, no. Y, pp. Z-Z, Month Year.

**Figures:**
- Upload separately
- Formats: TIFF, EPS, PostScript
- Resolution: High quality (300+ DPI)
- Black and white preferred (color charges apply)
- Captions: Required, detailed

**Tables:**
- Editable format
- Embedded in document
- Captions required

**Required Sections:**
- Abstract
- Keywords (at least 5)
- Introduction
- Methods/Methodology
- Results
- Discussion
- Conclusion
- References
- Acknowledgments (as footnote on first page)

**Page Limits:**
- Regular paper: Up to 12 pages (8 complimentary, 4 extra pages at $150/page)
- Survey/tutorial: Up to 12 pages
- Short paper: Up to 4 pages

**PRISMA Requirements:**
- Not explicitly required, but PRISMA compliance recommended for systematic reviews

**Special Requirements:**
- ORCID IDs required for all authors
- Manuscript received date (filled by editor)
- Author affiliations as footnote on first page
- Funding acknowledgment as footnote

**Open Access:**
- Hybrid journal
- Open access charge: $2,495 USD
- Traditional publication: No charge

## Common Requirements Across Journals

### Universal Requirements:
1. **Abstract**: All journals require abstract (structured or unstructured varies)
2. **Keywords**: Most require 5-10 keywords
3. **PRISMA**: Health journals strongly recommend/require PRISMA 2020 compliance
4. **ORCID**: Most journals now require ORCID IDs for all authors
5. **Funding**: Funding statement required
6. **Conflicts of Interest**: COI statement required
7. **Author Contributions**: Most journals require (CRediT format common)
8. **Data Availability**: Increasingly required

### Format Preferences:
- **Health Journals**: Prefer Word documents initially, format after acceptance
- **IEEE Journals**: Prefer LaTeX, especially for final submission
- **All Journals**: Accept PDF for initial submission

### Citation Styles:
- **IEEE**: Numbered with square brackets [1], IEEE format references
- **Health Journals**: Vancouver style (numbered), AMA style (JMIR)
